Inline Graphics Content Reuse

Overall goal is content reuse for graphics that are used thousands of times throughout the manual system.

  • These graphics are inline with the content, and generally denote regulation and aircraft effectivity or numbered steps in correlation with a graphic.
  • The graphics live in an article that can be viewed by any Technical Writer and easily inserted.
  • Before this icon bank, user would have to remember where an icon exists elsewhere in the manual, go to that chapter to copy and paste it.
  • This is an example of our content reuse for graphics. I directly implemented this change.
